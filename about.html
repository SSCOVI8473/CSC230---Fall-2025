About Me

My name is Steven Scoville, and I’m currently studying Information Technology with the goal of becoming a software developer. Before shifting into IT, I served four years in the U.S. Army as a paratrooper and Military Police and worked several security and dispatch positions. Those experiences gave me discipline, problem-solving skills, and a strong work ethic that I now bring into coding.

I’ve grown passionate about development through building hands-on projects in C#, HTML, and CSS. I enjoy figuring out how things work, fixing problems, and creating clean, functional designs. This site highlights my progress as I continue learning and developing my abilities.

Outside of coding, I spend a lot of time gaming, exploring new music, and occasionally picking up the guitar to learn something new. I also enjoy spending time with my family, which keeps me grounded and motivated as I work toward a future career in software development.
